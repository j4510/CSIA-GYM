Platform Features

Everything you need
to level up.

From isolated challenge instances to a ranked community — CSIA GYM is built for serious learners.

🎯
Multi-Category Challenges
Web exploitation, cryptography, reverse engineering, forensics, OSINT, binary exploitation, steganography, and more — curated by admins and the community alike.

Live Isolated Instances
Web and netcat challenges spin up your own sandboxed container on demand. Dynamic per-user flags. 15-minute sessions, extendable up to 60 minutes. No shared state.
Docker · nsjail · socat

Percentile-Based Ranking

Be the first to solve a challenge and claim First Blood. Gold, silver, and bronze positions are tracked per challenge and displayed on the solver leaderboard.

Getting Started

Zero to hacker
in four steps.

01
Create Your Account
Register in seconds. Your identicon avatar is auto-generated. No email verification required — just pick a username and start.
02
Pick a Challenge
Browse by category, difficulty, or source. Filter out solved challenges. Save ones you want to return to. Subscribe for solve notifications.
03
Hack & Submit
Launch your isolated instance, exploit the vulnerability, capture the flag in CSIA{...} format, and submit. First blood is tracked.
04
Climb the Ranks
Your composite score updates in real time. Earn badges, post writeups, vote on challenges, and watch your rank title evolve as you improve.
