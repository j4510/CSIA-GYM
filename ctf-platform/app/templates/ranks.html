HOW RANKS WORK

Your rank is calculated from a weighted score that reflects your overall contribution and skill across the platform. Scores are compared against all active players to produce a percentile — the higher your percentile, the higher your rank. Ranks update in real time as the player base grows.

1
Solve quality — Challenges solved weighted by rarity (fewer solvers = higher reward), difficulty (Easy / Medium / Hard), and point value. This is the highest-impact factor.
2
Category breadth — How much of each category you've completed. Solving across many categories rewards versatility.
3
Community posts — Every post you author contributes to your score. Quality engagement matters.
4
Accepted challenge submissions — Challenges you submitted that were approved by admins. A strong positive signal of expertise.
5
Rejected submissions — Submissions that were rejected carry a small negative weight. Quality over quantity.
6
Post upvotes received — Upvotes on your community posts reflect how valuable others find your contributions.
7
Comments made — Active participation in discussions adds a small boost.
8
Wrong flag attempts — A very small penalty per incorrect submission. Encourages careful thinking over brute-forcing. (Lowest weight of all factors.)
