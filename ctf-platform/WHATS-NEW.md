## Here's what's new with our current version: v2.0

* v2.0 (March 20, 2026)
    - Revamped website design.
    - Full-blown website walkthrough is now available.
    - File uploads are now available and limited to 250 MB per user.
    - Revamped the community wall.
    - Revamped the index page.
    - Reactions are now available for comments only.
    - Ranks are now attainable based on how you do on the gym. Click your current rank for more information.
    - A Graph Radar has been added to check your stats and also compare your stats to other users (maximum of 4 users to compare).
    - Phone users are now able to register, login, check the scoreboard, community, and account settings.
    - Flairs are now available on the community as well as post sorting.
    - Flags can now have RegEx for customization.
    - You can now deploy web-applications or static web files via .tar.gz.
    - Dynamic flags are enabled and cannot be disabled for Web Exploitation and Reverse Engineering challenges that have 'flag.txt'.
    - You can now deploy Linux ELF executables for challenges to be solved.
    - Administrators can now pin posts on the community wall.
    - You can now format your texts on the description of the challenges you're submitting as well as the communtiy posts.
    - You can now crop your uploaded pictures within the settings.
    - Enabled clipboard copy for social media links that does not have a URL.
    - Badges are now with borders signifying their tier.
    - Revamped administrator dashboard and functions.
    - Badges are now visible and has descriptions on how to earn. Click any available badge for more information.
    - Limited Edition badges now have a count.
    - Announcements can now be made by the Administrators.
    - Notifications are now enabled by default for all users. Disable it by going to your settings.
    - You can now save challenges for bookmarking.
    - You can now subscribe to a challenge which will notify you when people solve challenges you've bookmarked.
    - You can now subscribe to a post which will notify you when people replies to a post.
    - Bug reporting is now available.
    - Full-blown auditing is now enabled (it was previously only for administrators).
    - Created a new role: Community Moderator for managing community posts.
